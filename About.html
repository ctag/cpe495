Observations

We had huge success in keeping the cost of our project very low relative to similar retail products.

Our hardware is very helpful for many situations. By being a low-cost alternative to existing technology our hardware is increasingly available to users without insurance. This also benifits anyone considering developing this technology by allowing them to begin working on similar hardware with a much smaller financial investment.

The software is available as both a stand-alone application as well as a chrome extension. As a chrome extension it is easily accessable by many users with minimal computer knowledge required.
